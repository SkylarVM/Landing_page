The Problem

Studying alone can feel overwhelming, stressful, or simply boring. Many students lose motivation, lack a routine, or feel isolated during long study sessions. Traditional productivity apps are cold and impersonal — failing to emotionally support students during their academic journey.

The Arctiqa Solution

Arctiqa blends gamification, emotional support, and structured productivity into a single experience. Inspired by high-engagement apps like Finch, Duolingo, Fabulous, and Kahoot, Arctiqa creates a warm study environment where you’re never alone. Your companion — a baby Antarctic penguin — evolves alongside you, motivating you to stay consistent and making studying feel meaningful and fun.

Arctiqa: Your Penguin Companion

Born with You

When you start your first study session, your egg hatches — and Arctiqa the penguin comes to life. It marks the beginning of your shared journey.

Evolves Through Your Progress

As you study, complete routines, and stay consistent, your penguin grows from a baby into new stages, unlocking animations, accessories, and evolutions.

Study “Calls”

During sessions, Arctiqa appears in adorable animations — reading, typing, cheering, or focusing with you — making every study session feel supported and warm.
