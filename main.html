The Problem

Students often lose motivation, feel alone while studying, and struggle to maintain habits. Many productivity apps feel cold, overwhelming, or impersonal — making it hard to stay consistent and emotionally supported during stressful school or university periods.

The Arctiqa Solution

Arctiqa transforms studying into an emotional and gamified experience. Instead of facing your tasks alone, you gain a companion: a baby penguin who grows as you grow. Each study session becomes a shared moment of progress, encouragement, and bonding. Inspired by Finch, Duolingo, Fabulous, and Kahoot, Arctiqa combines science-backed productivity tools with warmth, playfulness, and emotional support.
