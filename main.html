The Problem

Students struggle with staying motivated, building consistent study habits, and managing distractions. Traditional productivity tools feel rigid or boring, making it hard to keep students engaged. High school and university students often need structure, encouragement, and a sense of progress to stay on track.

The Arctiqa Solution

Arctiqa transforms studying into a gamified experience. By blending elements from popular apps such as Finch, Duolingo, Fabulous, and Kahoot, Arctiqa turns productivity into a personalized adventure. Users level up, track progress, unlock rewards, and follow science-based routines — all while enjoying a friendly, supportive environment designed for real student life.

Who It’s For

High School Students

For students who want better focus, fun study habits, and a boost in consistency.

University Students

For those balancing classes, projects, and exams — and needing a tool that keeps them engaged.

Young Learners

Gamified tasks and rewards help younger students build strong long-term habits.

Expected Outcomes

Arctiqa is designed to increase motivation, build consistent study routines, and help students stay accountable in a fun and interactive way. While exact data will be collected during beta testing, our goal is to help students feel more organized, confident, and motivated every day.
